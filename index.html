About Smart City Hub

CitySync is a cutting-edge platform designed to empower councils and municipalities with tools and data for building the smart cities of tomorrow. By licensing councils to control and supply data, CitySync enables seamless integration with third-party applications while driving innovation and revenue opportunities for the future.

Key Features

Data Control

Empower councils to manage and distribute data securely while maintaining transparency and control.

Monetisation

Enable councils to generate revenue by licensing data to third parties and fostering public-private partnerships.

Smart Applications

Provide a solid foundation for smart city applications, including mobility, energy management, waste reduction, and more.
